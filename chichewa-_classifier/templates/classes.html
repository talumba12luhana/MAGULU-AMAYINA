The 9 Chichewa Noun Classes

Chichewa is a Bantu language that organises all its nouns into grammatical noun classes. Every noun class has a unique prefix pattern that governs agreement with verbs, adjectives, pronouns, and demonstratives throughout a sentence. Understanding noun classes is fundamental to grammatically correct Chichewa.
